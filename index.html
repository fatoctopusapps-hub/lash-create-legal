Lash Create

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 June 2026

This app is provided by Fat Octopus Apps. This Privacy Policy explains what information Lash Create handles and how.

Summary

Your photos and designs stay on your device — we have no servers or accounts of our own and never upload your images. The app does, however, include the TikTok Business SDK so we can measure how well our advertising performs. Through that SDK the app collects a limited set of device and usage data and shares it with TikTok. Details are below, and you can decline cross-app tracking when prompted.

What data we collect

Lash Create itself does not run its own servers, accounts, or analytics. The only data leaving your device comes from the TikTok Business SDK, used solely for advertising measurement and attribution. Through it we collect:

  • Identifiers — your device's advertising identifier (IDFA), available only if you allow tracking when prompted, plus a TikTok-assigned advertising/device identifier.
  • Purchase activity — when you start or complete a Premium subscription, the product, price, and currency of that purchase are reported as a conversion event.
  • Usage data — app installs, launches, and sessions (product interaction), used to attribute and measure ad campaigns.
  • Device & network information — standard device and connection details (such as device model, operating-system version, and IP address) collected by the TikTok SDK.

We do not collect your name, email, contacts, precise location, or the contents of your photos.

Advertising, analytics & tracking (TikTok)

We use the TikTok Business SDK to understand which of our ads lead people to install and subscribe, and to measure campaign performance. The data above is shared with TikTok for advertising attribution, measurement, and analytics, and may be used to track you across other companies' apps and websites for these purposes.

Because this involves tracking, iOS asks for your permission through the App Tracking Transparency prompt the first time you open the app. If you choose "Ask App Not to Track," your IDFA is not shared and TikTok relies on Apple's privacy-preserving measurement (SKAdNetwork) instead. You can change this any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

TikTok's handling of this data is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

How your photos are handled

When you take a photo with the camera or pick one from your photo library, the image is processed entirely on your device. Photos are never uploaded to a server.

Designs you save are stored locally on your device. If you choose to save a design to Photos, that copy is added to your iOS Photos library; nothing is sent anywhere else.

Face data

Lash Create uses Apple's on-device Vision framework (specifically VNDetectFaceLandmarksRequest) to automatically detect where each eye sits in a photo you take or select. The detection produces only 2D pixel coordinates that describe the position, approximate width, and rotation angle of each eye in the image.

No facial recognition is performed. No biometric identifiers (face descriptors, face vectors, faceprints, or any equivalent) are created, stored, or transmitted. The eye coordinates are used for one purpose only: to automatically place lash extensions on the correct position over each eye so you don't have to drag them into place manually.

The eye coordinates exist only in memory during the current editing session. They are not saved to disk, exported, transmitted to a server, shared with any third party, or used for any purpose other than positioning lashes. They are released from memory as soon as you close the editor, open a different photo, or quit the app.

If you save a design, the saved file is the composited image (your photo with the lashes drawn on it). The underlying eye coordinates are not included in any saved file.

Permissions

Lash Create requests the following permissions and uses them only for the stated purpose:

  • Camera — to capture a new photo for lash design.
  • Photos (Read) — to let you pick an existing photo from your library.
  • Photos (Add) — to save your finished designs to your iOS Photos library when you choose to.

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

In-app purchases

Lash Create offers an auto-renewable Premium subscription processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not see your payment information. Apple handles billing, receipts, and renewals. Your purchase status is stored on your device only.

Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children

Lash Create is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and the advertising measurement described above relies on tracking only where permission has been granted through iOS. If you believe a child has used the app, contact us and we will help.
