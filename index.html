Lash Create

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 May 2026

This app is provided by Fat Octopus Apps. This Privacy Policy explains what information Lash Create handles and how.

Summary

Lash Create does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything you do in the app stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

What data we collect

None. Lash Create does not collect any personal data, usage data, device identifiers, location, or contact information. We do not use analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking.

How your photos are handled

When you take a photo with the camera or pick one from your photo library, the image is processed entirely on your device. Photos are never uploaded to a server. The app uses Apple's on-device Vision framework to detect eye positions in your photo so that lashes can be placed correctly.

Designs you save are stored locally on your device. If you choose to save a design to Photos, that copy is added to your iOS Photos library; nothing is sent anywhere else.

Permissions

Lash Create requests the following permissions and uses them only for the stated purpose:

  • Camera — to capture a new photo for lash design.
  • Photos (Read) — to let you pick an existing photo from your library.
  • Photos (Add) — to save your finished designs to your iOS Photos library when you choose to.

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

In-app purchases

Lash Create offers an auto-renewable Premium subscription processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not see your payment information. Apple handles billing, receipts, and renewals. Your purchase status is stored on your device only.

Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children

Lash Create is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
